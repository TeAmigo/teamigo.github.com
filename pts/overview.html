Overview

The pts set of apps are what I use to practice my passion for predicting price direction of various financial instruments. Of late, the instruments of interest have been currency futures, and to a lesser degree, interest rate futures. Anyone interested in using the software, or playing with it for any purpose, feel free to contact me. It's been a solo venture, so haven't worried about others being able to understand the code.

I watch and jump in (or not) based on what I see in ptscharts. The quotes database is updated via ptsupdater, with 1 minute bars.
